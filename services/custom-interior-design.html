Types of Commercial Spaces

Commercial interior design encompasses a wide range of spaces, each with its own unique requirements and challenges. Office interiors, for example, need to balance functionality and aesthetics to the create a productive and inspiring work environment.

This often involves open-plan layouts, private offices, collaborative spaces, as well as features like ergonomic furniture, natural light, and wellness areas.
